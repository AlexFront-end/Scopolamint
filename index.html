Scopolamint is a modern advantageous preparate that is aimed to prevent and treat heartburn and other
symptoms caused by too much acid in the stomach (acid indigestion). Scopolamint is aimed for the treatment of
heartburn, ulcers, and prevention of gastric diseases. The main active ingredient is rabeprazole sulfone. The drug
has no contraindications and can be used by adults and children over 12 years. Released on prescription.

Side effects and Precautions

Rarely, headache or abdominal pain might occur. In such cases, it is strongly recommended to contact a doctor or
pharmacist promptly.

The list of rare side effects includes symptoms of a low magnesium blood level, signs of lupus, severe intestinal
condition, diarrhea, abdominal or stomach pain/ cramping fever.

Rarely, it might cause vitamin B-12 deficiency (unusual weakness, sore tongue, or numbness/ tingling of the
hands/feet).

The allergic reaction is extremely rare but might cause: rash, itching/swelling, severe dizziness, trouble breathing,
signs of kidney problems, such as a change in the amount of urine.

In all cases of side effects, it is strongly recommended the treatment course and contact a doctor.
